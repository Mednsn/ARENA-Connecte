Palworld Mobile Version Set for Official Reveal Soon
by Karen Castillo
Published 44 minutes ago
Fans of Palworld eager for news on its mobile version should mark November 13 on their calendars, as that's when Krafton will reveal its gameplay for the first time. Since its announcement in 2024, the mobile version of Palworld has been a hot topic in the community, with many speculating about what the port will look like.
Palworld Mobile Version Unveiling Date and Details Date: November 13 to 16 Location: BEXCO in Busan, South Korea
Fans outside South Korea may not be able to attend this year's G-STAR event, but they likely won't miss out. Krafton is expected to release a global teaser trailer for Palworld Mobile. Additionally, Korean outlets will likely cover the official unveiling, giving fans worldwide their first look at the anticipated port. A reassuring detail is that Krafton has not labeled the presentation at the upcoming gaming convention as a behind-closed-doors event, implying that press and visitors will be able to share what they see.
